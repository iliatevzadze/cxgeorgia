# Product Vision

## Georgian-first CX platform

Georgian CX Platform is a **Georgian-first unified customer experience platform** for local and regional businesses. It gives teams one workspace to manage customer support, messages, social complaints, reviews, phone-call logs, and analytics — without juggling Gmail tabs, Facebook inboxes, WhatsApp threads, spreadsheets, and disconnected tools.

The default language is Georgian (`ka`). English (`en`) is supported for mixed-language teams and international partners.

## Target local users

Initial focus: Georgian businesses that already handle support across multiple channels but lack a unified system.

| Segment | Typical pain |
|---------|--------------|
| IT support teams | Tickets scattered across email and chat |
| Hosting and domain companies | Repeated billing and DNS questions with no shared history |
| Google Workspace resellers | Account and license issues tracked in personal inboxes |
| SaaS support desks | Growing volume with no Georgian-localized tooling |
| SMBs | Gmail, Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, phone calls, and spreadsheets |

These teams need something **locally useful today**, not a generic global product translated as an afterthought.

## Regional long-term direction

The platform is built local-first but designed to scale across the South Caucasus and adjacent markets. Georgian language support, local business workflows, and regional channel patterns (social messaging, phone-heavy support) are first-class — not optional add-ons.

Long term, the same Universal Case model should support businesses in neighboring markets with additional locales and channel adapters.

## Why this is not a Zendesk clone

| Zendesk-style approach | Our approach |
|------------------------|--------------|
| Generic global helpdesk | Focused CX operating system for local businesses |
| Feature breadth over depth | Universal Case as the single mental model |
| English-first, localize later | Georgian-first from day one |
| Enterprise pricing and complexity | Zero-budget, local-first, grow with the business |
| Ticket = email thread | Case = any channel, one timeline |

We are not competing on feature checklists. We are building the internal operating system Georgian support teams actually need.

## Human-agent-first philosophy

Automation and AI may come later, but the product is designed around **human agents** doing real support work:

- Agents see full customer context, not isolated channel silos.
- Assignment, priority, and resolution are explicit — not buried in email threads.
- The UI prioritizes clarity and speed for daily agent workflows.
- Bots and AI assist agents; they do not replace the case model or agent accountability.

## Competitor pain points we address

| Pain point | How we address it |
|------------|-------------------|
| Channels live in separate inboxes | Universal Case unifies all channels |
| No shared customer history | Customer timeline with events and linked cases |
| Tools are English-only or poorly localized | Georgian-first UI via next-intl |
| Expensive per-seat SaaS | Local-first, zero-budget foundation |
| Over-engineered for SMB needs | Focused scope, phased delivery |
| Spreadsheet tracking | Structured cases with assignment, SLA, and resolution |
| Social complaints lost in feeds | Future channel imports map to cases |
